# Step 8 — Figure 4: focused residue-mapping figure

## Aim

This figure visually summarizes the two aligned residue positions that are most relevant to the functional interpretation of O67940_AQUAE relative to the chlorinase-related reference 2Q6O and the fluorinase reference 1RQP.

## Why this figure matters

Global structural similarity and overall sequence similarity are informative, but they do not by themselves determine precise enzyme specificity. The original paper emphasized residue-level comparison at positions linked to functional interpretation.

The focused residue summary identified two key aligned sites:

- Site 1: O67940 Val67, 2Q6O Thr70, 1RQP Thr75
- Site 2: O67940 Gly127, 2Q6O Gly131, 1RQP Ser158

These positions are more biologically informative than a generic similarity score because they connect the alignment directly to functional-site interpretation.

## What the figure shows

The figure displays the residues observed in the three proteins at the two aligned positions.

Interpretive value:

- At site 1, O67940 has Val67, whereas both reference proteins have Thr at the corresponding position.
- At site 2, O67940 has Gly127, matching the chlorinase-related reference residue Gly131 in 2Q6O rather than the fluorinase residue Ser158 in 1RQP.

## Interpretation

This figure supports a cautious interpretation. The residue-level evidence does not justify calling O67940 a fluorinase. Instead, the mapping is more consistent with the broader conclusion that O67940 is related to the SAM-dependent halogenase family and is more compatible with the original paper's cautious chlorinase-like/halogenase interpretation than with direct fluorinase-specific assignment.
